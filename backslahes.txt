\n - an escape sequence that denotes a newline character(parcel kursoru jauna rinda)
\t -used to shift the cursor to a couple of spaces to the right in the same line(parel kursoru uz tuvako tabulacijas poziciju)
\v -vertical tab(horizontali taja pasa pozicija, vertikali uz augsu, uz leju)
\b -backspace(parcel kursoru vienu poziciju atpakal)
\a -an ascii character, used to display a sound(atskano skanju)
\r -carriage return. Used to add a carriage return to text(parcel kursoru uz rindas sakumu)
